# Bear Hunt (Bear Trap) Guide

## Overview
Bear Hunt (Bear Trap) is an alliance damage event where members rally a giant bear and try to deal as much damage as possible within **30 minutes**. Rewards are based on **individual damage dealt**, so maximizing damage is the entire goal.

### Key Facts
- The event lasts **30 minutes**
- It must be started by an **R4 or R5** (alliance leadership)
- Players attack the bear using **rallies**
- **No troops die** and **no healing is required**
- Rewards depend on **how much damage you personally deal**

---

# Preparation Before the Event

## Explosive Arrowheads
Before the event begins, alliance members can donate an item called **Explosive Arrowheads** to the Bear Trap.

Players obtain Explosive Arrowheads by **killing beasts in the wilderness**. Donating these upgrades the Bear Trap and increases troop attack for the entire alliance.

Maxing the trap (**Level 5**) is one of the **largest alliance-wide damage boosts** available during the event.

### Bear Trap Enhancement Levels

| Level | Troop Attack Bonus |
|------|--------------------|
| Lv1 | +5% |
| Lv2 | +10% |
| Lv3 | +15% |
| Lv4 | +20% |
| Lv5 | +25% |

Encourage alliance members to **donate arrowheads before the event begins** so the trap reaches the highest level possible.

---

## Teleport Nearby
Players should teleport their cities **close to the Bear Trap** before the event begins.

### Why This Matters
- Shorter **march time**
- Your troops can reach rallies **faster**
- You can participate in **more rallies during the 30 minute window**

Organized alliances often create a tight circle of cities around the trap, sometimes called a **“Bear Hive.”**

This positioning significantly increases total alliance damage.

---

## Activate Buffs
Right before the event starts, activating buffs can noticeably increase damage.

### Common Useful Buffs
- **Pet Buffs**
  - Snow Leopard  
  - Cave Lion  
  - Snow Ape  
  - Rhino  
  - Sabertooth  

- **Minister Titles**
  - Minister of Defense → **+15% Lethality**
  - Minister of Strategy → **+8% Attack** and increased **Deployment Capacity**

### Optional Buffs (More Costly)
These buffs can increase damage further but are often used less frequently due to cost.

- **Troops Attack Up**
  - +10% Attack
  - +20% Attack

- **Troops Damage Up**
  - +10% Lethality
  - +20% Lethality

- **Deployment Capacity**
  - +10%
  - +20%

All of these buffs increase the **damage your army deals to the bear.**

# Combat Mechanics

## Rallies Overview
Players **cannot attack the bear alone**. Instead, players must participate in **rallies**.

### How a Rally Works
1. One player starts a rally on the bear
2. Other players join that rally
3. When the timer ends, everyone's troops attack together

### Rally Roles

**Rally Leader**
- The player who starts the rally
- Provides the main hero lineup for the attack

**Joiners**
- Players who join the rally leader
- Their troops help increase total rally damage

Because rallies combine many players into one attack, **joining rallies quickly and consistently is critical to maximizing damage.**

---

## Hero Skills
During the event, heroes and their expedition skills have a **major impact on damage output**.

Each rally consists of **three heroes** from the rally leader.

### Expedition Skill Rules

- The **rally leader contributes all expedition skills** from their heroes.
- **Joiners only contribute their first expedition skill.**
- Only the **first four joiners** with the **highest leveled expedition skill** will apply their skill to the rally.

### Why This Matters
The order in which players join rallies can affect total damage. Strong players with high expedition skills joining early can significantly increase rally damage.

For best results:
- Join rallies **quickly**
- Use heroes with **strong expedition skills**
- Strong players should try to **join early when possible**
